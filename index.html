Code Journal

First Session Mockups:

Sketch No.1:

When the mouse hovers over the picture, the head in the picture will separate from the body and spiral down onto a circular button on the controller. After this, the button will glow green along with the frame around the picture. The head will need to be separated through an image editing program such as Preview.

Sketch No.2:

When the mouse hovers over the picture, the throne from Game Of Thrones will appear behind the main subject of the picture. In order to do this, the main picture will have to be replaced with a picture in which the background of the original picture is removed, leaving the subject on his own. Then the picture of the throne will be translated upwards behind the subject so that it looks like the subject is seated on the throne.

Sketch No.3:

When the mouse hovers over the picture, a ball will appear in the picture from underneath it and then a foot will proceed to kick it after it too has appeared from underneath the picture. The ball fly off the card and the frame of the card will change into something with a net pattern.
